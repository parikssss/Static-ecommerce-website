Technology and its impact

January 25,2023

By Prasamsha Thapa

In essence, technology is a methodical approach to accomplish tasks or resolve issues. Technology is another human endeavor aimed at creating items that make life more comfortable for people, such as machinery, computers, household appliances, and other items. Technology also refers to new ideas, skills, methods, or ways of doing things that can lead to the creation of new objects.

It can also be described as a cultural tradition created in human groups for the advancement of the natural world. In fact, we use technology to manage the environment in which we live.

Technological implementations for the maintenance of bonsai plants.

Pruning
Branches are pruned with scissors and other tools to adjust the shape of the tree. A young bonsai tree being pruned for the first time requires particular care, since its basic shape and style will be decided then. When the tree gets a little older, branches are cut to achieve a better balance, taking into consideration things like the direction, density and length of the branches. A tree that is nearly fully grown is pruned to maintain its beauty.

Bonsai plants will expand if nothing is done to stop them. While pruning can assist manage a bonsai tree's size, its aim is not to prevent it from growing; rather, it is a method for utilizing plants' propensity for development to produce aesthetically beautiful shapes. The timing and manner of branch cutting varies depending on the type of plant. By giving bonsai plenty of sun and air, proper trimming can encourage its healthy growth.

Wiring
Wiring is used to make a desired turn or straighten an abnormal curve; wires are fastened to the trunk and branches. Copper and aluminum wires are frequently used; however, aluminum is simpler to handle and better for novices. The size and hardness of the branch determines which of the more than ten various wire thicknesses should be utilized. Although they occasionally just cover a tiny portion of the tree that needs to be changed, wires are typically wrapped around the trunk and then from the lower to the upper branches. When the wires are left on for an extended period, they may begin to bite into the growing branches and harm the plant. Before this occurs, bonsai growers must take care to remove the wiring. A tree's trunk and branches may be wired again if the desired effect isn't realized.

Positive impacts of technology Modern technology is the era in which we live. Education, communication, healthcare, agriculture, and social and personal life are all strongly impacted by technology. Technology has advantages and disadvantages just like everything else, yet we simply cannot manage without it. Consequently, it is essential that we use technology to enrich our life.

Some of the positive impacts of technology are mentioned below.

  1. Technologies are employed in the field of healthcare and medical research.
  2. Technology Has Enhanced Knowledge Available to Us.
  3. Technology Has Expanded Personal Productivity.
  4. Technology supports trade and business.
  5. Environmental studies advance thanks to technology.

Negative impacts of technology.
Technology has both beneficial and harmful impacts. There is no doubting that technology has considerably bettered our lives. However, it has some adverse effects that materially affect our lives. These negative impacts must not be disregarded. These are things we need to be conscious of.

  1. Reduced level of movement.
  2. A constant source of distraction is technology.
  3. Technology burns more energy.
  4. Technology makes people less empathetic.
  5. Resources Running Out

Impacts of technology while selling Bonsai plants.
Technology advancements have made it easier to plant, harvest, and even sell bonsai plants. The effects of technological transformation on the sales industry have been especially evident. Modern salesperson act in a very different way from their ancestors. They face new obstacles and have access to new materials, methods, and tools for bonsai plants.
Some of the impacts that technology have made while selling Bonsai plants are given below:

  1. The Capability to Identify, Assess, and Connect with Buyers globally.
  2. The ability to find almost any piece of information The capacity to sell anywhere you go.
  3. From a sustainability standpoint, technology evaluation for system sales is still in its infancy.
  4. The development of technologies on a worldwide scale that are used for sales but influence sustainability outside of agriculture is on the rise.

Overall conclusion:

Technological change has been the measure driving force for increasing selling productivity and promoting the development of agriculture. The choice of technologies and their adoption was to increase the production, vend and generate income. The concept of technology has contributed to the overall welfare for agriculture. Its impact is economically efficient and profitable, socially responsible, while it also improves environmental quality.
